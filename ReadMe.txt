The BLAZE Retail wordpress plugin displays your dispensary’s menu and allows new and existing users to place an order. It’s simple to use and extremely efficient for consuming new orders and members. 

Major features in BLAZE Retail include:


- Mobile friendly 
- Shows automatic updates to your dispensary menu
- Customizable messaging in the widget allows you to ensure your dispensary by-laws are communicated
- Sign up process for new members
- Sends all orders and new members to the BLAZE Retail platform for approval.
